Extraction

Extraction: is a 2020 American action thriller. Announced to be in development, the film is a direct adaptation of the 2014 graphic novel as the Russo Brothers intended to bring the story to the screen. Several deviations separate the film from the source material of the graphic novel, however the overall story is largely maintained

Release Year:2020

Starring: Chris Hemsworth, Rudhraksh Jaiswal, Randeep Hooda, Priyanshu Painyuli.

Director: Sam Hargrave

Plot: While battling his own demons, accepts the offer of rescuing an Indian crime lord's teenage son from Bangladesh.

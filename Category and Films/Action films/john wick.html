John wick

John wick: is a 2014 American action thriller film written by Derek Kolstad. Lionsgate Films purchased the distribution rights to the film two months before its release date on October 24, 2014. Following a successful marketing campaign that changed its perception from disposable entertainment to a prestige event helmed by an affable leading actor

Release Year:2014

Starring: Keanu Reeves, Michael Nyqvist, Alfie Allen, Adrianne Palicki.

Director: Chad Stahelski

Plot: John Wick, a retired hitman, is forced to return to his old ways after a group of Russian gangsters steal his car and kill a puppy gifted to him by his late wife.

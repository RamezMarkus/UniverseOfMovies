Pandemic

Pandemic: is a 2016 American science fiction thriller film written by Dustin T. Benson. Rachel Nichols stars as a doctor who leads a group to find survivors of a worldwide pandemic. The film is shot in a first-person POV, similar to first-person shooter video games.

Release Year:2016

Starring: Rachel Nichols, Alfie Allen, Paul Guilfoyle, Pat Healy.

Director: John suits

Plot: When a virus takes over the entire planet, a scientist and her team battle hordes of bloodthirsty zombies in Los Angeles, where they run a search-and-rescue mission for uninfected survivors.

Red notice

Red notice: is a 2021 American action comedy film written and directed by Rawson Marshall Thurber and it became the 5th most-streamed movie title of 2021. Two sequels are in development and will be filmed back-to-back with Johnson, Reynolds, Gadot, and Thurber expected to return.

Release Year:2021

Starring: Dwayne Johnson, Ryan Reynolds, Gal Gadot, Ritu Arya.

Director: Rawson Marshall Thurber

Plot: In the world of international crime, an Interpol agent attempts to hunt down and capture the world's most wanted art thief.

The hangover part II

The hangover part II: is a 2011 American comedy film produced by Legendary Pictures and distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures. The sequel to the 2009 film The Hangover and the second installment in The Hangover trilogy

Release year: 2011

Director: Todd Phillips

Plot: After the bachelor party in Las Vegas two years ago, Phil, Stu, Alan, and Doug head to Thailand for Stu's wedding. However, things go awry when Stu's soon-to-be brother-in-law goes missing.

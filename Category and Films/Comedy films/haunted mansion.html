Haunted Mansion

Haunted Mansion: is a 2023 American supernatural horror comedy film produced by Walt Disney Pictures and Rideback, it is the second film adaptation of Walt Disney's eponymous theme park attraction, following the initial one.

Release date:2023

Starring: LaKeith Stanfield , Tiffany Haddish , Owen Wilson , Danny DeVito , Rosario Dawson , Dan Levy , Jamie Lee Curtis , Jared Leto.

Director: Justin Simien

Plot: A woman and her son enlist a motley crew of so-called spiritual experts to help rid their home of supernatural squatters.

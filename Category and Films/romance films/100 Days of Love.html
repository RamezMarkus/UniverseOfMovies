100 Days of Love

100 Days of Love: is a 2015 Indian Malayalam-language romantic comedy film written and directed by debutant Jenuse Mohamed.[1] The film stars Dulquer Salmaan in a dual role, alongside Nithya Menen in the lead roles.[2] The film released worldwide on 20 March 2015.[2] It received mixed reviews from critics.[3][4][5] Dialogues were written by Duo writers M R Vibin and Suhail Ibrahim. Production controller is Sanjay Padiyoor

Release year: 2015

Director:Jenuse Mohamed

Plot: Balan K. Nair, who calls himself BKN, is a reporter in a famous daily based in Bangalore. After insulting his ex-girlfriend on Facebook in a drunken state, Balan feels like his life is not in sync. He is the "loser" of his family, since his brother Rocky is a successful doctor.

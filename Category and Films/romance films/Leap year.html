Leap year

Leap year is a 2010 romantic comedy film directed by Anand Tucker and written by Harry Elfont and Deborah Kaplan. Leap Year stars Amy Adams and Matthew Goode. The plot revolves around Anna Brady (Adams), who decides to travel to Dublin to propose to her boyfriend on leap day, as Irish tradition allows.

Release Year:January 8, 2010 (USA)

Starring:Amy Adams,Matthew Goode,Adam Scott,John Lithgow

Director: Anand Tucker

Plot:Boston real estate stager Anna Brady (Amy Adams) is frustrated that her boyfriend has still not proposed after four years, even though they are about to move into an upscale apartment together. She decides to travel to Dublin, where he's attending a cardiology conference.

Me before you

Me before you is a 2016 romantic drama film directed by Thea Sharrock in her directorial debut and adapted by author Jojo Moyes from her 2012 novel of the same name.

Release Year:June 3, 2016 (UK)

Starring: Emilia Clarke,Sam Claflin,Janet McTeer,Charles Dance,Brendan Coyle

Director: Thea Sharrock

Plot:Louisa "Lou" Clark is hired as carer for Will Traynor, a once successful banker and active sportsman now tetraplegic after being hit by a motorcycle. Will's mother hopes Lou's bubbly personality will lift Will's depressed and cynical spirits.

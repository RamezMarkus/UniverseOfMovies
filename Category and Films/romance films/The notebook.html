The notebook

The notebook is a 2004 American romantic drama film directed by Nick Cassavetes, from a screenplay by Jeremy Leven and Jan Sardi, and based on the 1996 novel of the same name by Nicholas Sparks.

Release Year:December 15, 2004 (Egypt)

Starring:Ryan Gosling,Rachel McAdams,James Garner,Gena Rowlands

Director: Nick Cassavetes

Plot:At a modern-day nursing home, the elderly Duke reads a romantic story from a notebook to a female patient: In 1940, at a carnival in Seabrook Island, South Carolina, lumber mill worker Noah Calhoun sees 17-year-old heiress Allison "Allie" Hamilton, there for the summer.

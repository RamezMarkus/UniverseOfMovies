Doctor who the day of the doctor

Doctor who the day of the doctor: 3D version. The Doctors embark on their greatest adventure in this 50th Anniversary Special: in 2013, something terrible is awakening in London's National Gallery; in 1562, a murderous plot is afoot in Elizabethan England; and somewhere in space an ancient battle reaches its devastating conclusion.

Release Year:2013

Starring: Billie Piper, Ingrid Oliver, David Tennant, Tom Baker.

Director: Nick Hurran

Plot: The Eleventh Doctor and Clara Oswald are brought by military organisation UNIT head Kate Stewart to the National Gallery. In the midst of the Time War between the Time Lords and the Daleks, the War Doctor decides to trigger an ancient and sentient weapon called the Moment to destroy both sides.

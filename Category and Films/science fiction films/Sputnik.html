Sputnic

Sputnic: is a 2020 Russian science-fiction horror film directed by Egor Abramenko in his feature directorial debut.

Release Year:2020

Starring: Oksana Akinshina, Fyodor Bondarchuk, Pyotr Fyodorov, Anton Vasiliev.

Director: Egor Abramenko

Plot: A psychiatrist, who uses controversial methods, is sent to an isolated military facility to examine an astronaut. Soon, she discovers that an alien lives within the latter.

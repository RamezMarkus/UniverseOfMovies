Sunshine

Sunshine: Sunshine is a 2007 science fiction psychological thriller film directed by Danny Boyle and written by Alex Garland. Taking place in the year 2057, the story follows a group of astronauts on a dangerous mission to reignite the dying Sun.

Release Year:2007

Starring: chris evans, cillian murphy, Michelle yeoh, Rose byrne.

Director: Danny boyle

Plot: In the year 2057, the Sun is dying and Earth is on the threat of a second ice age. Eight international astronauts command the Icarus II, a ship fronted with a mirrored stellar bomb designed to reignite the dying star.

Cowboys And Aliens

Cowboys And Aliens: Jake, an amnesiac, enters the town of Absolution, which is ruled by the ruthless cattle baron Colonel Dolarhyde. When attacked by alien invaders, Jake becomes the town's only hope for survival.

Release Year:2011

Starring: Daniel Craig, Harrison Ford, Olivia Wilde, Sam Rockwel.

Director: Jon Favreau

Plot: In 1873 New Mexico Territory, a man awakens injured in the desert with a strange metal bracelet attached to his left wrist and no memory. He wanders into the town of Absolution, where preacher Meacham treats his wound. Sheriff John Taggart recognizes the stranger as wanted outlaw Jake Lonergan and attempts to arrest him. Jake nearly escapes, but a woman named Ella Swenson interferes.

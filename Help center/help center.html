Help center

  • How can I stream movies on your platform?

    To start streaming movies on our platform, simply create an account or log in if you already have one. Once logged in, browse our extensive catalog, select the movie you'd like to watch,
    and hit play!

  • I'm experiencing playback issues. What should I do?

    If you encounter any playback issues such as buffering, freezing, or poor video quality, try the following troubleshooting steps:
    - Check your internet connection.
    - Refresh the page or restart the app.
    - Clear your browser's cache and cookies.
    - Make sure your device's software is up to date.

    If the problem persists, please reach out to our support team for further assistance.

  • Can I download movies for offline viewing?

    Yes, depending on your subscription plan and the availability of the movie, you may have the option to download it for offline viewing. Look for the download icon
    next to the movie you want to save, and follow the prompts to download it to your device.

  • Do you offer subtitles or dubbing for international movies?

    Yes, we strive to make our movies accessible to a global audience by providing subtitles in multiple languages and, in some cases, offering dubbed versions. You can usually adjust the language and subtitle settings directly on the video player.
